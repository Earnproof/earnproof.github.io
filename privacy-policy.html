Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

Short version: I don't collect personal data unnecessarily, I use Google Analytics to see how many people visit, and I'm transparent about it. That's basically it.

1. What Data Do We Collect?

Learn To Work is pretty simple. When you visit the site, here's what happens:

Google Analytics: I use Google Analytics to understand how many people visit, which pages are popular, and where they come from (which country, device type, etc.). This helps me know what content to write more of. Google collects this automatically via a tracking code on the site. You can read Google's privacy policy to see exactly what they do.

Email Contact: If you email me at hello.littleboom@gmail.com with questions or feedback, I'll see your email address and message. I only use this to respond to you. I don't sell it or use it for marketing.

No Forms, No Signups: Learn To Work doesn't have email signup forms or contact forms. There's no database of users. I don't collect names, emails, or other personal info unless you voluntarily email me.

2. Cookies & Tracking

Google Analytics uses cookies to track your visit. These are small files stored on your device. If you don't want this, you can:

  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Install a browser extension that blocks Google Analytics
  • Use private/incognito browsing mode

3. Third-Party Services

Learn To Work links to other websites like YouTube, Upwork, Amazon, etc. When you click external links and visit those sites, they have their own privacy policies. I'm not responsible for how they handle your data. Always read their privacy policies before signing up.

Affiliate Links: Some links are affiliate links. This means if you click and make a purchase, I might earn a small commission. But the affiliate programs (Amazon, ShareASale, etc.) have their own privacy policies about tracking.

4. Data Security

Learn To Work is hosted on GitHub Pages, which is secure. Your visits to the website are encrypted (HTTPS). However, if you email me or contact third-party services, those communications have their own security standards.

5. International Users & Privacy Laws

Learn To Work is accessible worldwide. Different countries have different privacy laws:

  • Pakistan: Data is subject to Pakistan's data protection laws
  • EU/GDPR: If you're in the EU, you have rights under GDPR
  • USA/CCPA: California residents have rights under CCPA

Since I don't collect personal data except for voluntary emails, most privacy laws don't heavily apply. But I respect your privacy regardless of where you are.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what data is collected: Mostly just analytics, which Google handles
  • Opt-out of tracking: You can disable Google Analytics tracking
  • Request deletion: If you've emailed me, I can delete your email
  • Ask questions: Email me if you have privacy concerns

7. Changes to This Policy

If I update this privacy policy, the changes take effect immediately. I'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top. Check back occasionally to stay informed.

8. Contact

If you have privacy questions, concerns, or want to request data deletion:

Email: hello.littleboom@gmail.com

Bottom line: I care about your privacy. I don't collect more data than necessary, and I'm transparent about what I do collect.
