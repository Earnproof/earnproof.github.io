How to Withdraw Money on ySense.

Admin: EarnProof Team | Updated: May 3, 2026

Paisa Nikalna (Withdrawal Intro)

ySense se apni earnings nikalna aik intehai seedha process hai. Pakistan mein rehne wale users ke liye ye guide bohot zaroori hai kyunke yahan hum Payoneer aur JazzCash ka sahi rasta batayenge taake aapka paisa secure rahe aur kam se kam tax kategi.

Step 1: Cash Out Feature Tak Rasayi

Sab se pehle apne dashboard par jayye aur top menu mein 'Cash Out' ke button par click karein. Yahan aapko mukhtalif payout options nazar ayenge jaise Skrill, Payoneer, aur Amazon Gift Cards.

Note: Pakistan ke liye hum hamesha Payoneer suggest karte hain kyunke iska rate sab se behtar milta hai.

💡 Pakistani Earning Hack:

Ek baar paise Payoneer mein aa jayein, to aap JazzCash app khol kar 'Payoneer' icon par click karein aur apna balance foran PKR mein withdraw kar lein. Ye instant aur sab se sasta tareeqa hai.

Step 2: Account Verification

Jab aap pehli baar withdrawal request karenge, ySense aapki security ke liye aapke mobile number par aik OTP code bhejay ga. Wo code enter karte hi aapki request submit ho jayegi.

Yaad rakhein ke aapka Payoneer account pehle se ySense ke sath link hona chahiye aur dono ka naam (Name) aik jaisa hona zaroori hai.

Aam Sawalat (FAQ)

Q: ySense se paise kaise nikalein?

A: Aap Cash Out section mein ja kar Payoneer ya Skrill select karke apni raqam nikal sakte hain.

Q: Kya JazzCash mein withdrawal mil sakta hai?

A: Ji haan, Payoneer account ko JazzCash app se link karke aap direct paise hasil kar sakte hain.
