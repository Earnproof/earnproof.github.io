💬 What is an AI Chatbot?

An AI chatbot is a smart software powered by artificial intelligence that communicates with users. It can answer questions, guide customers, and automate tasks in real time.

Unlike old bots, modern AI chatbots understand natural language. This means they can reply like humans and provide meaningful responses.

Example: A customer asks “What is your price?” → chatbot replies instantly → business saves time and increases conversions.
